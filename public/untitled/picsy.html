Personal Information Curation System

2015

PROBLEM

We have lots of calendar apps and weather apps, but none of these apps curate information actively. To get the most from time management apps, we are required to spend too much time managing them.

SOLUTION

Picsy curates the information it presents based on a user’s own actions. This is based on circumstances; when a user leaves one place and arrives at a destination, Picsy will organize their schedule and prepare notification for their next event.

Picsy curates information based on context.

As soon as the user arrives at a scheduled destination, their schedule is updated with the next event notification.

Picsy coordinates with Tile (a bluetooth device) and other wireless sensors, to find specific items related to user events.
